Final Project

The final project combines elements from Project 1 and Project 2, bringing together repeated imagery, color experimentation, and pattern design. Large-format printing and the laser cutter were used to build the physical structure of the piece. Adobe Fonts helped shape the typography, especially the choices placed in the top left.
